About Our Youth

Who We Are

ST JOSEPH'S MBIUNI YOUTH is a vibrant Catholic youth community that brings together young people who desire to grow spiritually, serve others and build lasting friendships founded on Christian values.

Inspired by the life of Saint Joseph, our patron saint, we strive to live lives of humility, responsibility, faithfulness and dedication to God's mission.

Through prayer, Bible sharing, choir ministry, retreats, charitable works, sports, leadership training and community outreach, we nurture young people into responsible Christians and future leaders of the Church and society.

Our youth fellowship welcomes every young person with love and encourages everyone to discover and use their God-given talents for the glory of God and the growth of His Church.

🎯

Our Mission

To unite young people in Christ through worship, service, leadership, fellowship and evangelization while strengthening their Catholic faith.

🌟

Our Vision

To build a vibrant generation of faithful, responsible and compassionate Catholic youth committed to serving God, the Church and society.
